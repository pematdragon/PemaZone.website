Muay Thai

Muay Thai — the art of eight limbs — emphasizes kicks, knees, elbows, and clinch work. I focus on:

  • Basic stance and footwork
  • Jab-cross-kick combinations
  • Clinch control and knee strikes

Training Tips

  1. Warm up, skip rope, and shadowbox for 10–15 minutes.
  2. Practice pad work focusing on timing and power.
  3. Cool down and stretch to prevent injury.

Boxing

Boxing builds speed, precision, and head movement. Key areas I train:

  • Jab and cross mechanics
  • Footwork and angles
  • Defense: slips, blocks, and parries

Drills

  1. Focus mitt rounds (3-minute rounds).
  2. Footwork ladders and cone drills.
  3. Sparring control: light contact to learn timing.
